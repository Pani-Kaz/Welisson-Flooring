What are the advantages of hardwood flooring compared to other types of flooring?
Hardwood flooring offers durability, sophistication, and timeless appeal. It can be easily renewed through sanding and refinishing, and it increases the resale value of a property, making it a popular choice for high-end homes.
Is vinyl or laminate flooring resistant to moisture?
Vinyl flooring is highly resistant to moisture, making it suitable for kitchens and bathrooms. Laminate flooring is less moisture-resistant but can handle minor spills if cleaned quickly.
Is porcelain tile suitable for outdoor areas?
Yes, porcelain tile is durable and weather-resistant, making it an excellent choice for outdoor areas like patios and walkways.
